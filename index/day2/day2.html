KABADDI

Kabaddi is a contact team sport played betweeen two teams of seven players, originating in india the objective of the game is for a single player on offence,referred to as araiderrorun into the opposing teams half of the court touch out as many of their players as possible and return to thir own half of the court all without being tackled by the defenders in 30 seconds.pointsare scored for each player tagged by the raider while the opposing team earns a point for stopping the raider.
